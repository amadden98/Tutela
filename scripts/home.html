Supporting Your Mental Health

We're here to help you help yourself.

Tutela is here to help you learn about and navigate the various mental health resources available at Duke University. We offer a few services.

Find the Right Care

There are so many on-campus mental health resource options, which can be more confusing and intimidating than helpful. To guide you through initiating care, we developed a survey that will suggest three options to fit your needs and lifestyle.

Student Testimonials

Students have a tough time talking about mental health, especially about their symptoms and the care they are receiving. We want to destigmatize your experiences with mental health care while providing an outlook on how that resource may apply to you.

Onboarding Information

Once you've narrowed down your options or chosen a resource, view the onboarding information to understand your next steps for initiating care. There are closed-captioned videos as well as links to their corresponding websites.
