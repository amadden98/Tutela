About Tutela

Our Mission

Tutela is here to help you learn about and navigate the various mental health resources available at Duke University. As senior Duke students, we recognize that finding mental health care is a problem many of our peers face and wanted to create a one-stop resource for students to find their best care resource.

Why Tutela?

“Tutela” is a Latin word meaning care, safeguard, and support. We hope our website will support our users in finding meaningful care.

Our Team

This website was created by 3 electrical and computer engineering students for a course called Human Centered Computing. The A Team consists of Angie Lei (Pratt '21), Amanda Madden (Pratt '20), and Ana Martinez (Pratt '21).

Tutela 1.0

This website was created as a prototype for an ECE class and has the current functionality: survey to help students find their top care option matches and a chatbot to answer questions about our website as well as suggest ways to aid common mental health symptoms. In future work we hope to add content to our "Student Testimonials" and "Onboarding Information" pages.
